    St.Umar Hr. Sec School, Indore is one of the Successfully Running Institutions of Karimia Society, Indore established in the year 1980. it is an English Medium School, recognized by M.P Government, running classes from Nursery to Twelfth with the strength of 4000 students under the guidance of 200 Qualified and dedicated staff personnel.

    it has always followed the divine path of the society and has outstood the session with the shining result in Hr. Secondary and High School exams.

    It has an adequate facility of advanced computer and Science Lab. Along with various sports activities like Fencing, Archery floor Ball, Aerobics, Marshal Arts, Scout, and Guide activity etc.supported by an updated Collection of books in the library

    The school has been catering the students hailing from the various strata of the society since its inception. The school excelled not only in academic but also in other faculties

    Facilities


    • Separate Shift for Girls and Boys From Nursery to 12th Std ,M.P Board.

    • For Girls Dini Taleem Through Alima.

    • Project Based Education from 1 li.

    • Well Equipped Science and Computer Lab.

    • Smart Classes

    • From Class 11th onwards (Science,Maths Biology,Commerce,Commerce with I.P. Faculty)
